Friction Welded Nozzle Part Manufacturing Project

If the PDF failed to load, refresh or download to view here: Download PDF

Objective

The project's goal was to design and engineer a nozzle part for friction welding, incorporating an injection molding process. This design, with the tongue and groove joint , was injection molded in Solidworks to simulate the proccess and to identify the ease or difficulty, along with the injection times and other insightful parameters like the central temperature or the shear stress at the end of the fill .

Software Used

Onshape for CAD design and SolidWorks for simulation of the injection mold and the determination of optimal injection sites.

Skills Learned

CAD modeling for friction welding, injection mold design, and analytical techniques for simulation verification in SolidWorks.
