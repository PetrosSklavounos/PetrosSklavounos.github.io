Custom Ring Casting Manufacturing Project

Objective

to design, simulate, and manufacture custom metal rings via investment casting. The process encompassed initial CAD design, wax 3D printing, assembly into a tree structure for casting, and final post-processing for quality finishing.

Software Used

Onshape for CAD design and SolidWorks for simulation of the investment casting process and determination of optimal injection sites.

Skills Learned

The project honed skills in 3D design, simulation analysis, wax modeling, and the investment casting process, including mold creation and metal pouring techniques.
