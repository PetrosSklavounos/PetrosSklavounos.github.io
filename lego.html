Learning Solidwork's Parts and Assembly through Lego Design Project

Objective

The objective was to model at least 2 parts in 2 weeks that can be assembled. Solidwork's drawing feature was then used to develop dimensioned drawings of a three view and isometric drawing for each part, an isometric drawing of the assembly, and the exploded view of the assembly.

Project Description

The three parts that I chose were the torso, head and legs of a Lego figure as at the time that is what an assembly most resembled.

Software Used

This project was developed using Solidworks.

Skills Learned

This project taught me to properly interface with Solidworks by utilizing tools like mirroring features, loft, and extrude cut by surfaces. Planes were also necessary.
