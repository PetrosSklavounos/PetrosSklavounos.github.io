Mechanics of Materials- Frame Design for the Cooper Hyperloop team

Objective

Optimize a design for a frame that is structurally sound and meets the design objectives set for the Cooper Hyperloop team.

Software

Onshape 3D CAD for modelling, nsys Workbench for Finite Element Analysis,to observe axial forces due to braking force of the pod's Eddy Current Brakes.

Skills Learned

We learned to apply beam theory from Mechanics of material to a real-world scenario - the forces experienced by the metal frame of a hyperloop pod as it travels down a track.

Real-world Application

This research was then used for the Cooper Hyperloop Team's Design Package submission to the Canadian Hyperloop Committe along with the other subsystems' work, which is outlined in the pdf below.
